| T-09-03 | Repudiation / trust | app/(marketing)/page.tsx copy | mitigate | Use only accurate claims already true per STATE.md (BYO-SMTP, AES-256-GCM at rest, verify-before-send); no fabricated testimonials or metrics (Copywriting Contract) |
