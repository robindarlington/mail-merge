<objective>
Lift the proven merge-and-send logic out of the existing CLI (`send-credentials.ts`) into pure, reusable `lib/core` modules — generalizing and fixing it along the way per CONTEXT.md's Claude's-discretion decisions. Three fixes-by-design land here: (1) `fill()` generalized from hard-coded `{{email}}/{{password}}` to arbitrary `{{column}}` keys and applied to BOTH subject and body (EDIT-03, fixes the CLI subject bug); (2) CSV parsing swapped from the naive split-at-first-comma to papaparse (CSV-02); (3) the SMTP transport takes an EXPLICIT `secure` boolean instead of inferring `port === 465` (the CLI anti-pattern).

Purpose: This engine is the synchronous test-send path (Phase 5) and the background worker's send loop (Phase 6). Pure modules (no DB/Clerk/Next) keep it trivially testable now and reusable later. Done as a TDD plan because fill and csv have crisp input to output contracts.
Output: `lib/core/fill.ts`, `lib/core/csv.ts`, `lib/core/send.ts`, a barrel `index.ts`, and unit tests for fill (subject+body) and csv.
</objective>
